Chapter One: Revolution

What do we do?

We bring the big screens into Virtual Reality Cinematography has gone through countless of technological improvments since its birth in 1896 by Auguste and Louis Lumière but the concept of passively staring at a 2D screen has not changed.

We want to cast you into your favorite movies using Virtual Reality!

Using modern day Virtual Reality headsets, Google Jump's Video Stiching technology, as well as our team's experience in both filmmaking and VR development
lumièreVR is pioneering the art of Virtual Reality Film Production.

Immersive and Interactive

Withlumière stories live through the eyes of your favorite characters, or interact with the very world of your imaginations.

Write your own stories

lumière platform will allow for User Generated Content to be posted, upvoted, and selected to be forged into an all authentic VR Story

Humble Beginnings

lumièreVR will be beta testing in Downtown Las Vegas We aim to contribute to the downtown project by expanding the already booming entertainment and tech scene in downtown Las Vegas. We believe the foottraffic and culture in Vegas is exactly what we need to grow organically.

Future

Experience seamless and immersive Virtual Reality with our VR theater rooms. With sensors at every corner, the entire room can be plugged into our virtual content, allowing you to explore the virtual world with no fiction. We believe the future of film to be interactive rather than passive.
